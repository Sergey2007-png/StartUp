What problems does creating a website solve?

Without a website, you miss out on potential customers. A professional website helps you establish an online presence and attract new clients.

Customers trust companies with professional websites. A website boosts trust in your brand by providing all necessary information about your services and products.
Limited Marketing Opportunities

Development stages

Business Analysis and Strategy

The first step is to analyze the niche, competitors and target audience. This helps to determine the structure of the site and its functionality, taking into account the needs of users and the characteristics of the market. The data obtained forms the basis of the product development strategy.

Prototype development

Before creating the interface, we develop a prototype of the project, experimenting with the arrangement of blocks and elements. We cooperate with the client, coordinating ideas and taking into account wishes. The sketch should convey the general structure, logic and functionality.

Site design

We develop UX/UI for the site - we create an interface based on a prototype. The UI developer works on the visual style, the UX developer optimizes the user experience, improving the convenience and speed of interaction.

Website programming

Programming is first done by Frontend development, which works on design implementation, loading speed and adaptation. Further Backend development improves the internal part of the site, is responsible for quality, performance and security.

Testing

This is a test of the site's functionality and usability before launch. Website testing allows you to identify errors and shortcomings in the developed website in order to correct them before the site becomes available to users. Testing is carried out using automatic and manual methods, as well as special software.

Upload to hosting and support

After completing the project, we publish and test the site, making sure it is correct and fast, set up analytics for monitoring and provide free technical support for a month to make changes and adjustments.
